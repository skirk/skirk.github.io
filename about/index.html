About

Frequently asked questions:

Q: “Hi, what’s your name?”

A: My name is Tuomo. I come from Finland.

Q: “Cool! What do you do?”

A: I am about to finish my studies in Computer Graphics.

Q: “Plimey, how did you end up studying that?”

A: I have always been keen on pretty pictures. I have big interest in comics and animation, hence I ended up studying Computer Animation & Visualisation in Bournemouth. There I picked up interest in programming things and that instantly seemed like a fun thing to do. After my undergrad I felt an itch to do a bit more of studying and rolled up on Masters by Research course, which meant me joining one of the research groups in the university. With the group I conducted some research about shape modelling, in which my role was to look into efficient methods to sample Functionally Represented models. In this work I was focusing mainly on parallel computing.

Q: “… Okay, what are you up to next?”

A: Right now I’m looking for a job. Software development would be the field, but visual effects are no problem either.

Q: “Nice, what else do you do, as in hobbies and such?”

A: I’m interested in many things, I try to balance between technical and art stuff so that if my job gets really technical, in my spare time I end up doing some drawing and so forth, and vice versa. On top of that I’m really keen on hiking and I tend to do some long hikes at least once a year, and multiple daily hikes a year. Lately I’ve also been going to some climbing sessions, it is really good fun.
