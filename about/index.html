About

People sometimes come to me with questions “So, what do you do?” And my answer usually boils down to “I’m a programmer.” That’s not entirely true. I do all sorts of creative stuff that I happen to find interesting at the minute. This blog features some of these things.

If people were to ask me “What do you do for living?”, then answer would certainly be; yup I’m a programmer. But that’s not entirely true either; I’m actually doing research. My field of research is Function Represented models and their visualisation.

When I’m not doing the things above, I’m either doing rock climbing or searching for the meaning of life. Books are the usual suspects when I’m hunting for the answers. The most satisfying answer I got so far is 42.
